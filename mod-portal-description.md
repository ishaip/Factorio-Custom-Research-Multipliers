# Custom Research Multipliers

Fully configurable research cost multipliers with decimal precision. Two systems: **category multipliers** scale cycle counts, **per-pack multipliers** scale packs per cycle.

🔴🟢⚫🔵🟣🟡⚪ — Every science pack gets its own slider.

Supports **Space Age**, **Space Exploration**, **Krastorio 2**, and any other mod — science packs are detected automatically. Configure in **Settings → Startup**.

Forked from [Research Multipliers](https://mods.factorio.com/mod/research-multipliers) by Xevion.
